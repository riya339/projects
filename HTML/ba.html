Journalism and Mass Communication

Introduction


Journalism and Mass Communication is amongst the choicest undergraduate courses for those wanting to make a career in media. A wide range of universities offers this
program to instill interested students with the requisite knowledge and skills to explore the domain of mass media. Journalism and Mass Communication encompass the
varied types of mass media that are being utilized for the dissemination of news in contemporary times. This blog aims to bring you a comprehensive guide on Bachelor
of Journalism and Mass Communication, its syllabus and subjects, scope as well as top universities you can choose from.

Duration: 4 weeks

Syllabus


Week 1:

Fundamentals of Communication Skills,Fundamentals of Journalism ,Print Media ,
Media Law and Ethics

Week 2:

Basics : Radio Broadcasting, Fundamentals of Computers ,Advertising ,Reporting
Writing for Media ,Editing, Information Society,Media Criticism

Week 3:

Basics: Photojournalism ,Filmmaking,English Literature ,Magazine Media

Week 4:

Basics: Cinematography Skills ,Public Relations ,TV Broadcasting ,Cinematography Skills
